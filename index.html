This research develops a platform for investigating computer vision-based bridge inspection algorithms, termed Random Bridge Generator (RBG). The RBG produces synthetic environments with six different types of bridges (slab bridges, beam bridges, girder bridges, arch bridge, cable-stayed bridges, and suspension bridges) and their structural components that are of utmost interest according to the field inspection standards. The synthetic bridges are generated randomly, following relevant design codes and standards.
